01

Arrival

Monday, August 17th
early morning travel, evening by the water
morning
4:30 am — the bus leaves Ohrid as the sky is still dark. A taxi from Ksamil to the ferry, then the crossing over the Ionian. By afternoon, Corfu. Check in, drop your bags, let the island find you.
afternoon
how to spend the first afternoon

Our base is Villa Kefalomandouko — on a hill just outside Corfu Town (~5 min by car to the centre, ~10 min to the port). Times below are rough one-way trips from the villa.

🏛
Old Town wander
Lose yourself in the Venetian laneways of Kerkyra — past crumbling archways, bougainvillea, and the sound of somewhere old. ~8–12 min scooter · centre.
🌊
Old Town Beach
Faliraki / NAOK area — a town swim without leaving Kerkyra. ~10–14 min · east side of town.
☕
Rest & coffee
Find a café on the Liston, order something cold, and just sit. After 4:30 am, you've earned the stillness. ~8–12 min · Liston.
⛪
Kanoni & Mouse Island
The postcard view — Vlacherna Monastery, Pontikonisi, runway over the water. ~12–15 min · south of town.
🌳
Mon Repos park
Shaded estate walks and a small quiet beach — gentle first dip. ~10–14 min · east of town.
🚶
Garitsa Bay
Long waterfront promenade — golden hour, sailboats, tavernas. ~8–12 min · south harbour.

02

Beach Day

Tuesday, August 18th
sun, salt, nothing to do but be warm
morning
After breakfast, we pick up the scooter — then the beach is yours to choose.
pick your beach

Approx. one-way scooter times from Villa Kefalomandouko (hill near Corfu Town — hotel quotes ~5 min to the centre by car). Traffic and stops add a little.

🏰
Old Fortress beach
Swim inside the Old Fortress walls — stone, history, calm water. ~10–14 min · Corfu Town.
🏖
Dassia
Long shallow bay, family-friendly — watersports and tavernas behind the trees. ~15–18 min · northeast coast.
🪨
Barbati
White pebbles, glass-clear water, Pantokrator rising behind you. ~22–26 min · northeast.
🛵
Glyfada Beach
Wide sandy arc, cliffs at the edges — among Corfu's best west-coast swims. ~25–30 min · west.
🌅
Myrtiotissa
Secluded cliff-backed sand below Glyfada — quiet and wild (part naturist). ~26–32 min · west.
🌴
Kontogialos
Golden sand below Pelekas — sunset colours and a chilled beach bar scene. ~24–28 min · west.
⛰
Agios Gordios
Dramatic bay framed by cliffs — long beach, tavernas, sunset vibe. ~26–32 min · southwest.
🌿
Paleokastritsa
Six bays, boat trips into the caves, monastery on the hill — the west coast's headline act. ~30–36 min · west.
🪁
Halikounas & Issos
Endless sand meeting Lake Korission — dunes, kite-surfers, wild open horizon. ~38–45 min · south.

03

Boat Trip

Wednesday, August 19th
out on the Ionian, chasing blue lagoons
all day
choose your voyage

Most group cruises leave from Corfu Town port — ~10–15 min scooter from the villa; hotel suggests ~5 min taxi to the port if we're lazy.

🏝
Paxos & Antipaxos
Full-day classic — sea caves, swimming stops, Voutoumi's blue. Boards near the port · ~8 NM south.
💎
Blue Lagoon cruise
Speedboat / rib trips to turquoise pools off the mainland coast — lots of operators; compare times at the port.
🇦🇱
Saranda day trip
Ferry from Corfu to Albania — seafront Saranda, optional Butrint ruins (passport required). Full day.
🌊
Northeast coast by boat
Cruise the east — Agni, Kalami, hidden bays; sometimes wine stop. Upmarket, slower pace.
🛶
Sea kayak / snorkel
Half-day paddles in sheltered bays — often Mon Repos / Garitsa / east coast. Meet point usually ~10 min from the villa.

04

Make Something

Thursday, August 20th
hands in clay, roads through olive groves, one last dinner
morning
yoga

Optional start to the day — heart one if you want it on the itinerary.

🧘
Yoga studio (town)
Drop-in or booked class at a Corfu Town studio — mats, props, cool studio air after warm nights. ~10–15 min scooter from Villa Kefalomandouko.
🪷
Garden yoga with Lukas
Slow flow or stretch on the grass under the olives — just us, no packing up to leave. A private lesson at Villa Kefalomandouko, taught by me.
for your hands

Craft & food experiences — drive times from Villa Kefalomandouko unless noted.

🏺
Pottery workshop
Hand-building & glazing at a local studio — leave with your own piece. Venues vary; often ~15–25 min from town.
🫒
Governor olive mill
Award-winning Governor estate — groves, tasting, deep dive into Corfiot liquid gold. ~25–30 min · east coast hills.
👩‍🍳
Greek cooking class
Market shop then cook — pastitsada, tzatziki, island wine. Classes usually central Corfu · ~10–18 min.
⛰
Mt Pantokrator
Highest peak — monastery café, 360° views over Albania & the Ionian. Drive most of the way · ~45–55 min to summit area.
afternoon
on the scooter

Afternoon loops — all times one-way from the villa.

🛵
Pelekas village
Hilltop cafés, Witch House ceramics, beaches below (incl. Kontogialos). ~22–28 min · west.
📷
Lakones & Bella Vista
Cliff village above Paleokastritsa — famous lookout over the bays. ~30–36 min · west.
🌺
Achilleion Palace
Sisi's palace — statues, terraces, gardens tumbling down the hill. ~25–30 min · east of Corfu Town.
🦩
Korission Lagoon
Nature reserve — flamingos, dunes, Issos beach at the sea. ~38–45 min · south.
